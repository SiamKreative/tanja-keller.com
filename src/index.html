about

Tanja Keller is a German born stunt woman, action performer and actress.

Trained since the age of 7, Tanja is an accomplished Martial Artist, winning numerous titles in forms and fighting all over the world.

In 2014, whilst performing on live shows across Europe, she gained her first film credit in the action movie "Ultimate Justice" as a stunt performer. The following year she relocated to Thailand to pursue her passion, and has already appeared in several feature films, and commercials - either as a stunt double/performer or in speaking roles. Some of her recent credits include “Hard Target 2” & "Knights".

Tanja is trained in Sport Karate, Kickboxing, Muay Thai, BJJ and Tricking. She has experience with wires, high falls and various styles of choreography. She speaks German, English and Thai.
